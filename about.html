About Me


My name is Adam Nedens, and I am currently a senior at the University of Jamestown
in Jamestown, North Dakota. I am studying computer science and play on the golf team.


I first got into photography around high school, when I would take pictures on my iPhone on vacations.
When the Covid-19 pandemic arrived, I decided to experiment with cameras and began to use my mom's old
Canon EOS Rebel T4 camera. This camera helped me understand the basics of photography and how I can have
more control over my photos. I hope to continue taking pictures after graduation and
expanding my knowledge of photography, along with expanding my outreach to more people.


Thank you for taking the time to read about me and my website!
